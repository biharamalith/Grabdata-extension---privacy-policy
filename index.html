Grabbit Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Grabbit is designed to run locally in your browser. It extracts data from the page you choose and gives you export files on your device.

Data We Collect

Grabbit does not send your scraped content, browsing history, or personal data to our servers. The extension processes page content locally in your browser and downloads the results to your computer.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No account information is required.
  • No scraped page content is uploaded to external servers.
  • No browsing history is transmitted for analytics or advertising.
  • No personal data is sold or shared with third parties.

Permissions Used

Grabbit may request browser permissions such as active tab, scripting, downloads, and tabs so it can read page content and create export files. These permissions are used only for the extension’s local extraction features.

Third-Party Services

Grabbit does not rely on third-party analytics, tracking, or data processing services for your scraped content.

Children's Privacy

Grabbit is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy if the extension changes. The latest version will be published at this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact the developer using the support email or contact method listed in the Chrome Web Store listing.

Plain-language summary: Grabbit runs locally in your browser and does not send scraped data, browsing history, or personal information to external servers.
